01 — About

Engineering reliable systems, from the database to the dashboard.

I'm a Java Full Stack Developer with around seven years building robust, high-performance enterprise applications across banking, financial services, public sector, retail, and fintech. I'm primarily a backend engineer — roughly 75% backend, 25% frontend — most at home designing microservices and the event-driven plumbing that keeps them honest.

On the backend I work deep in Java 8/11/17, the full Spring ecosystem, REST & GraphQL APIs, and messaging with Kafka.
